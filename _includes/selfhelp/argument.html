1. The category "self-help" or "self-improvement" covers too much for comfort.
  2. Better to split it into at least 4 classes, with different goals and criteria:
  3. "Lifehacks": conscious changes to your environment or actions. Optimising.
  4. "Attempts": things you try out or perform without understanding fully. Generative.
  5. "Techniques": conscious mental exercises to change your mindset. Deliberative.
  6. "Seeking": mental exercises with a focus on emotion or phenomenology, not very analysable. Contemplative.
