## Breakthroughs


* I was disappointed in last year's malaria vaccine (RTS,S) but [R21 might be the real deal](https://www.thelancet.com/journals/laninf/article/PIIS1473-3099(22)00442-X/fulltext): best guess is ~80% protection (nonrobust, sample had very few deaths). way more scalable too. One of humanity's oldest and most pitiless enemies is on his way out.
